About Commercial International Bank (CIB) in Egypt

Website

Commercial International Bank (CIB) is Egypt's leading and largest private-sector bank, offering comprehensive financial products and services across multiple banking segments. The bank serves over 500 of Egypt's largest corporations, along with enterprises of all sizes, institutions, high-net-worth individuals, and retail customers. CIB has established itself as the most profitable private sector bank operating in Egypt, with a network exceeding 180 branches across the country.
